. . . An Explanation . . .

VidVoid is a media playlist creation app. When you find yourself in need of a new movie or TV show, VidVoid is here! Explore the cosmos of media entertainment with our ". . . Fill The Void" feature, which will provide a nearly endless but ever-expanding universe of suggestions.

But if you feel the pull of an old memory among the stars, use our "Search The Void . . ." feature to find the tale that's calling to you, whether it be a TV show, movie, or actor/actress.

On your cosmic journey, add any result to a personal list that will persist even after you leave our galaxy.

Lastly, e-mail custom lists to anyone. Share your discoveries, your suggestions, or your favorites. Create a scary movie list or a must-see list and send it to friends, family, or anyone you might want to share a bit of the media galaxy with.

Use the above exploration tool to return to the top of the screen if needed or to minimize the menu. It will follow you on any search . . .

This app is powered by The Movie DataBase API and The emailJS API.
